Experience

For as long as I can remember, I’ve always been what some would label a fitness junkie. I’ve competed in a variety of team sports, run a number of marathons, and even snagged a few medals in bodybuilding competitions. From a professional standpoint, I earned a master’s degree in movement science and sports management from the School of Kinesiology at the University of Michigan, I have published a handful of graduate research papers in the areas of sports performance, I’ve worked as an athletic trainer for a variety of professional sports teams including the Chicago Cubs (MLB), the New England Patriots (NFL), and the Chicago Cubs (MLB), the New England Patriots (NFL), and the Colorado Avalanche (NHL).
